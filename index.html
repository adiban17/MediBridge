Speak your mind, naturally.

A seamless, voice-activated interface designed to bridge the digital divide. By eliminating the need to type or read complex terms, it ensures accessible mental health care for every user, regardless of technical literacy. Powered effortlessly by the Bhashini API.

Unbroken connection, anywhere.

Built to thrive on the most fragile mobile networks, this highly compressed conferencing tool guarantees stable audio and video. It eliminates dropped calls and frozen screens, ensuring care reaches the most remote areas without interruption, leveraging WebRTC (AV1/Opus Codecs).

Your records, always ready.

A resilient, offline-first database that securely stores patient history locally. Even in areas entirely cut off from cellular signals, health workers can log prescriptions and review data, syncing automatically the moment a network returns, driven by PouchDB and CouchDB.

Pharmacy routing, automated.

Save time, travel, and frustration. Our intelligent alert system instantly communicates with nearby pharmacies to verify medication availability before you ever leave your home. Seamlessly connected through the WhatsApp Business API.
